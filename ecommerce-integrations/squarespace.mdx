title: "Squarespace"

---

To start using our Squarespace integration, you need to first register your Squarespace application, and then enter your Client ID and Client Secret on the Integration Labs dashboard or use the Integration Labs Credentials.

These steps are outlined in greater detail below.

---

### Step 1 - Register your Squarespace App

1. Go to [https://partner.squarespace.com/oauth-form](https://partner.squarespace.com/oauth-form)
2. Fill up and submit the form. For the ‘Redirect URIs’, enter [ROOTFI CALLBACK URL](/api-reference/v3/overview/environments#callback-urls)
3. Once you submit the form, you will receive an email from Squarespace with the client ID and client secret in the next few days.

## Step 2: Enter Your Client ID and Client Secret on the Integration Labs Dashboard

1. Login to the Integration Labs dashboard.
2. Select the 'Integrations' tab on the sidebar - here you will see a list of platforms you can integrate with.
3. Click the 'Set Up' button on the Squarespace card.
4. When the form opens up simply enter the 'Client ID' and 'Client Secret' that you generated and click 'Save'.
