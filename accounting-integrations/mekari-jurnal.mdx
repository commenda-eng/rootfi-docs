In order to get started using your own credentials for our Mekari Jurnal integration, you will need to first create your Mekari Jurnal application. After that, you will need to enter your Client ID and Client Secret on the Integration Labs dashboard.

These steps are elaborated upon in detail below.

---

### Part 1: Get API Key

1. Login to your Mekari Jurnal dashboard.

2. Click on the company dropdown and click on the `API Credentials` option.

![](/images/mekari_jurnal/api_cred.png)

3. You will find your API Key on this page.

![](/images/mekari_jurnal/api_key.png)
