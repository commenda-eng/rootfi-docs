---

If you wish to set up Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central on the Integration Labs Portal, you will need to follow the steps outlined below.

<Note>You will need to install Integration labs extenion to fetch some additional data.</Note>

<Steps titleSize="h2">

<Step title="Navigate to the extenions page" stepNumber="1">

Seach and Navigate to the extensions page 
![](/images/msdBc/manage-extenions.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Upload the extension" stepNumber="2">
1. Download the extenion from this drive link: [Link](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1r5WpQIP0t4ewIN37ShKe5WQpbs1ctwf2/view?usp=sharing)
2. Click on the 'Upload Extenion' option on the 'Manage' Dropdown on the Extensions Page.
![](/images/msdBc/upload-extenion.png)

3. Now click the 3 dots on the Select app file on the Upload Extenions popup. Upload the .app file downloaded from the drive link, check the "By deploying this extension..." radio button and click on deploy.
![](/images/msdBc/accept-terms.png)
Now the extension will be installed on your Business central tenant. To check the status of the installation navigate back to the Extensions page and click on manage. On the manage dropdown, click on the "Installation Status" option.
![](/images/msdBc/status.png)
You will find the installation status of the extension here.
![](/images/msdBc/status-view.png)

</Step>


</Steps>
