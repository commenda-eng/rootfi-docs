---

If you wish to set up Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central on the Integration Labs Portal, you will need to follow the steps outlined below.

<Note>You will need to install Integration labs extension to fetch some additional data.</Note>

<Steps titleSize="h2">

<Step title="Navigate to the extensions page" stepNumber="1">

Seach and Navigate to the extensions page 
![](/images/msdBc/manage-extensions.png)

</Step>

<Step title="Upload the extension" stepNumber="2">
1. Download the extension from this drive link: [Link](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1BFXkNI_9SwejUMGCSPj4qI1TxeB9kwsU?usp=sharing)
2. Click on the 'Upload Extension' option on the 'Manage' Dropdown on the Extensions Page.
![](/images/msdBc/upload-extension.png)

3. Now to upload, click the icon on the right of the "Select App" file. Upload the .app file downloaded from the drive link, ensure "By deploying this extension..." is enabled and click on 'Deploy'.
![](/images/msdBc/accept-terms.png)
Following installation, to check the status of the installation, navigate back to the Extensions page and click on 'Manage'. On the manage dropdown then click on "Installation Status" option.
![](/images/msdBc/status.png)
You will find the installation status of the extension here.
![](/images/msdBc/status-view.png)

</Step>


</Steps>
