In order to get started with our Qoyod integration, your customer will need to first create an API Key for their Qoyod application. After that, they will need to enter their API Key on RootFi when prompted during the Qoyod integration setup on RootFi's Connect Link.

These steps are elaborated upon in detail below.

---

### Part 1: Get API Key

1. Login to your [Qoyod Dashboard](https://www.qoyod.com/ara/%D8%AA%D8%B3%D8%AC%D9%8A%D9%84-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AF%D8%AE%D9%88%D9%84/).

2. Navigate to General Settings under Setting on the sidebar. Click 'Generate New Key' and click 'Save'.
